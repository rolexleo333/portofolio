8. Conclusion

Based on our analysis, there are some conclusions.
  • Most customers are female.
  • Favorite items of female customers are clothing products, while male customers are electronic products.
  • Most of the customers are aged 20 to 60 years.
  • The highest revenue was received in May 2023.
  • The best-selling products are clothing products.

8. Recommendation

Based on our analysis, there are some conclusions.
  • In this company, there are only 3 product categories with revenues that are not too far apart. This proves that the products in the company are quite attractive to customers. Therefore, the company can increase the product categories offered, so that it can create greater opportunities to get revenue from customers.
  • From the analysis, it is known that the least number of customer age categories is under 20 years old. The company can attract customers at that age by providing discounts by shopping with a student card.
  • The decline in revenue mostly occurs at the end of the first, second, and third quarters. The company can provide coupons with a certain minimum spending requirement, which are drawn quarterly to stabilize revenue in each quarter.
